🍂Seasons & Giants

A full year is 72 days. Each season brings unique hazards, resource shifts, and a boss Giant.

🍁 Autumn

Days 1–20 · ★☆☆☆ Difficulty
  • No hazards — safe exploration window
  • Goals: Science Machine, Alchemy Engine, Crock Pot, 20+ Berry Bushes
  • Prep: Thermal Stone, Winter Hat, food stockpile by Day 12
  • Lightning Rod is essential — craft it now

🐻 Bearger

3000 HP · Drops Thick Fur
Feed 10 Honey to hibernate. Drops Fur for Hibearnation Vest & Cat Cap.

❄️ Winter

Days 21–36 · ★★★☆ Difficulty
  • Freezing deals rapid damage — Thermal Stone is non-negotiable
  • Beefalo Hat (best) or Winter Hat + Wilson's beard
  • Walrus Camps active — farm MacTusk for Tam o' Shanter & Walking Cane
  • Rabbit holes close — rely on Crock Pot and Drying Racks

🦌 Deerclops

2000 HP · Drops Eyeball → Eyebrella
Spawns Day ~30. Leave base when stomping starts. Eyebrella = best rain/cold item.

🌧️ Spring

Days 37–56 · ★★☆☆ Difficulty
  • Constant rain → Wetness → Sanity drain. Use Eyebrella or Parasol
  • Bees hostile all season — stay away from hives
  • Beefalo in heat — attack on sight, avoid
  • Frog Rains can happen — run indoors or fight at walls

🦢 Moose/Goose

3000 HP · Drops Down Feather
Won't attack unless provoked. Feather → Luxury Fan (Summer cooling) + Weather Pain.

🔥 Summer

Days 57–72 · ★★★★ Difficulty
  • Overheating = rapid damage. Endothermic Fire for heat, not light
  • Combustion — flammable objects randomly catch fire
  • Ice Flingomatic is essential — covers your base against fires
  • Many players retreat to Caves — no seasons underground

🐉 Dragonfly

2750 HP · Drops Scales
Spits lava. Drop 20 Ash to pacify temporarily. Scales → Scaled Furnace/Chest/Floor.

⚠️ Monster Lasagna: 2+ Monster Food items = −20 HP, −20 Sanity. Never use more than 1 Monster Meat per recipe.

❤️Survival Mechanics

Health, Hunger, and Sanity — understand all three or die.

❤️ Health

  • Spider Gland — +8 HP
  • Healing Salve — +20 HP (3 Rocks + 2 Ash + 2 Spider Glands)
  • Honey Poultice — +30 HP (2 Papyrus + 1 Honey)
  • Pierogi — +40 HP (best food heal)
  • Fishsticks — +40 HP (1 Fish + 1 Twig + 2 filler)
  • Jerky — +20 HP + Sanity. 20-day shelf life
  • Cooked Blue Mushroom — +20 HP but −15 Sanity
💀 Resurrection: Touchstone (once per map, 50/50/50 stats), Meat Effigy (−30 max HP while placed), Life Giving Amulet (full stats, worn)

🍖 Hunger

  • Drains 75 points/day at standard rate
  • At zero: −1.25 HP every 5 seconds
  • Wolfgang: 1.5–3× drain depending on form
  • Wes: 1.25× drain rate
  • Sleeping costs ~60 Hunger
  • Top priority foods: Meaty Stew (+150), Bacon & Eggs (+75), Meatballs (+62.5)
💡 Once you have Crock Pot + Berry Bushes, hunger is trivially easy. The real killers are Hounds, bosses, and bad seasons.

🧠 Sanity

  • Below ~80: screen distortions, shadow creature silhouettes
  • Below 15% of max: Shadow Creatures spawn and attack
  • Drains: Darkness (−50/min), nearby monsters, rain, raw Monster Meat, graves
  • Restores: Flowers (+5), Tam o' Shanter (+6.7/min), Jerky (+15), Taffy (+15), Green Mushroom Cooked (+15), Tent (+60), prototyping (+15)
  • Shadow Creatures: Terrorbeak 300 HP/40 dmg, Crawling Horror 900 HP/20 dmg
  • Defeat them for Nightmare Fuel — needed for magic items

🌲Biomes & World

Each biome has distinct resources and dangers. Explore the full map in the first 3–5 days before settling.

Base Location Strategy
💡 Ideal base location: Near a Beefalo herd (wool, Hound defense), adjacent to Rockyland (Gold, Flint, Nitre), Grassland nearby (Grass, Saplings), and a Pond (Frog Legs, water fishing).
⚠️ Avoid: Building in Swamp (Tentacles destroy structures), on Beefalo (Spring mating = instant death), or in open Desert (constant Hound ambushes).

🧠Strategy & Tips

Hard-won wisdom from The Constant. What separates the living from the skeletons.

Early Game Roadmap

🗺️ Days 1–5: Explore

  1. Craft Axe + Pickaxe immediately
  2. Collect 40+ Grass, 40+ Twigs, 10+ Flint, 5+ Gold
  3. Make a Garland (12 Petals) for free Sanity
  4. Map the coastline — find Beefalo, Rockyland, Pigs
  5. Use Torches at night to keep moving

🏠 Days 5–12: Settle

  1. Build Science Machine at chosen base location
  2. Fire Pit, Crock Pot, 2× Chests, Backpack
  3. 4× Drying Racks and 4× Rabbit Traps
  4. Alchemy Engine — Lightning Rod immediately after
  5. 20+ Berry Bushes transplanted and fertilized

❄️ Days 12–20: Winter Prep

  1. Craft Thermal Stone (heat near fire, carry warmth)
  2. Winter Hat or Beefalo Hat for insulation
  3. Stockpile 10+ Jerky and 20+ Meatballs
  4. Build Prestihatitator if possible
  5. Locate Walrus Camp for Deerclops fight prep

⚔️ Combat: Kiting

  1. Attack once, walk away before enemy swings
  2. Wait for wind-up animation, walk in, attack again
  3. Spiders: 1 hit, dodge. Hounds: 1 hit, dodge
  4. Deerclops: 2–3 hits, then dodge the slam
  5. Always wear Football Helmet + Log Suit in combat

💀 Most Common Deaths

  1. Darkness — always carry 2 Grass + 2 Twigs
  2. Unprepared Hound attack — equip armor at first warning
  3. No Winter prep — Thermal Stone + clothing by Day 12
  4. Deerclops at base — leave when stomping sounds start
  5. Sanity spiral — keep hat equipped, eat Jerky, pick Flowers

🕹️ Mobile / Pocket Tips

  1. Red = attack, Green = interact, Orange = cycle action
  2. Kiting is harder on touch — armor is even more critical
  3. Use Tooth Traps instead of walls — easier to place
  4. Save frequently — crashes happen, autosave is at dawn
  5. Includes full RoG content (Wigfrid, Webber, 4 Giants)
End-Game Goals (100+ Days)
🏆 After Day 100: Ruins diving (Thulecite gear), Adventure Mode (5 chapters, unlock Maxwell), Kill all Giants, Mega base, Wes challenge run.
